What is Freediving?

       

Freediving is a form of underwater diving that relies on a diver's ability to hold their breath until resurfacing rather than using breathing apparatus such as scuba gear.

   
   
       

Why Freediving is Great

       

Freediving offers numerous benefits including improved lung capacity, mental clarity, and a unique connection with the underwater environment. It's a fantastic way to stay fit and experience the beauty of the ocean.
